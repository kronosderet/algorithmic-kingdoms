1. MAP SCALE BY DIFFICULTY
   Smaller map and shorter campaign for easy, larger map and longer story for hard.
   The dimensions are binary (128×128) so the scale is easy — 64×64 easy, 128×128 medium, 256×256 hard.
   Fewer incidents required on easy already exists, but physical map size should shrink too.

2. !! EXPLAIN BASIC CONTROLS ON START !!
   No tutorial, no hints. Game 3 she ran 4 workers on wood for 5 minutes and built nothing.
   At minimum: first-run overlay or progressive hints ("Click Town Hall to train units",
   "Build a War Nexus before the first attack", "Right-click to move selected units").
   Could tie into the "mechanics unlock at natural breakpoints" vision from above.

3. VISUAL CONFIRMATION OF COMMANDS (sound later)
   When you issue a command (move, gather, build, train), there's no feedback that it registered.
   Need: click marker on ground for move, gather icon flash, build placement sound, train queue pulse.
   Visual first, sound layer added later with the procedural audio system.

4. FORMATION DISCOVERY MUST BE AN EVENT
   She completely lost her first discovered resonance — the notification disappeared and she didn't
   understand what happened. Discovery needs to be a major event:
   - Camera pause/slow-mo moment
   - Persistent UI element showing what was discovered
   - Maybe a short "formation preview" animation
   - Discovery log entry in the message history

5. LOG OF SYSTEM MESSAGES (color coded)
   No message history. Notifications flash and vanish. Need a scrollable message log panel
   (collapsible) with color-coded entries: white=info, gold=discovery, red=attack, green=economy.
   The event_logger already tracks everything — just needs a GUI surface.

6. FIX EDGE SCROLLING — AREA TOO THIN, BOTTOM PANEL TRIGGERS IT
   Edge scroll margin is 15px (EDGE_SCROLL_MARGIN). Moving the mouse to the bottom panel
   or minimap area triggers unwanted camera panning. Jarring and frustrating.
   Fix: increase margin to ~25px AND exclude the bottom panel rect and minimap rect from
   edge scroll detection. Also exclude top bar.

7. MAIN TAKEAWAY: FOCUS ON UX AND USER JOURNEY
   The game has deep systems but the surface is hostile to new players. The whole experience
   from "start game" to "survive first incident" needs to be a guided journey.
   This aligns with vision note #2 above: "open with simple shape, deepen as you progress."

8. LIKES: fractals, originality, Tree of Life development idea
   The visual identity lands. The Mandelbrot menu, fractal buildings, algorithmic art —
   all good. Tree of Life as the expanding hub building resonates (pun intended).
   This is our differentiator. Double down on it.

9. RAINBOW UNIT COLOR SCHEME — 7 COLORS FOR 7 UNIT TYPES
   Current colors: workers blue, soldiers red, archers green — too easy to confuse with enemies.
   New scheme based on 7 rainbow colors (for the future 7 player unit types):
